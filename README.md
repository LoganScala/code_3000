## Ethics Statement
I think ethics mostly comes down to how we treat things that can actually feel or think for themselves. As tech gets better at appearing like humans through things like fake memories or really realistic reactions it gets harder to just say something is "just a machine." To me, being ethical means having enough empathy to recognize when something has its own identity, even if it was manufactured. The real test of our character isn't how we treat people like us, but how we handle things that sit in that weird gray area between human and program.
